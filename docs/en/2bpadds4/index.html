Heads Up

This is an early prototype of the site, lots of stuff is broken and somethings are out of date. That's the joy of buliding in public.

Overview

  • [x]

    Images placed in the `images`` folder are available to be called in `-- image`` sections with just their name (i.e. the full path to the image is not needed)

  • [x]

    You and make sub-folders in the `-- images`` directory and put images in them however you want. The builder will find them and provide the proper path to the templates for use

  • [x]

    Images can be called with or without their extension

  • [x]

    Alt text is pulled in properly
