    Overview

    Inline HTML tags don't convert to HTML in any section other than `-- html``. This proposal makes sure they don't break when they do show up and are simply passed through to the template

    • []

      Individual greater than and less than signs don't cause the builder to freak out

    • []

      Greater than and less than signs can be touching a word and still work (e.g. in HTML tags)

    • []

      Greater than and less than signs can be floating by themselves and not break
